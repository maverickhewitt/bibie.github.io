ADVERTISING

What is the Major Types of Advertising

1. Institutional Advertising
2. Product Advertising

What is Institutional Advertising?

Also known as corporate advertising, it promotes the corporation as a whole and establishes, changes, or maintains the corporation's identity.
It aims to maintain a favorable attitude toward the advertiser and its goods and services without asking the audience to take specific actions.

What is Product Advertising?

Promotes the benefits of a specific good or service.
The type of product advertising used (pioneering, competitive, or comparative) often depends on the product's stage in its life cycle.

Type of Product Advertising:
Pioneering Advertising

Aims to stimulate primary demand for a new product or product category. It is heavily used during the introductory stage of the product life cycle.

Type of Product Advertising:
Competitive Advertising

Employed when a product enters the growth phase of its life cycle, and other companies start entering the marketplace. It focuses on the unique selling propositions of the product to compete effectively.

Type of Product Advertising:
Comparative Advertising

Directly or indirectly compares two or more competing brands based on specific attributes.
It highlights the advantages of the advertiser's brand over others in the market.
