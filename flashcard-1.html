LO 1:
THE ROLE OF PROMOTION

What is the Role of Promotion in Marketing

Promotion is one of the four elements of the
marketing mix, along with product, price, and place.

What is the Role of Promotion in Marketing

Promotion refers to the various methods used by a company to communicate with its customers and persuade them to buy its products or services.

What is the Role of Promotion in Marketing

The role of promotion is to inform, persuade, and remind customers about a company's products or services, with the ultimate goal of increasing sales and profits.
