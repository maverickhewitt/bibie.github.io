LO 9:
PERSONAL SELLING

Advantages of Personal Selling

1.Provides detailed explanation or demonstration of complex or new products.
2. Sales message tailored to individual customer motivations and interests.
3. Targets qualified prospects, reducing wasteful promotion.
4. Cost control by adjusting the size of the sales force incrementally.
5. More effective than other forms of promotion in achieving sales and customer satisfaction.
