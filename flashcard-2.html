THE GOALS OF PROMOTION

What is the
primary goals of promotion?

inform, persuade, and remind customers about a company's products or services.

What is Informing?

Convert an existing need into a want or to stimulate interest in a new product.
It is generally more prevalent during the early stages of the product life cycle.

What is Persuading?

Designed to stimulate a purchase or an action.
Persuasion normally becomes the main promotion goal when the product enters the growth stage of its life cycle.
