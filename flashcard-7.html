LO 7:
PUBLIC RELATIONS

List the Major Public Relations Tools

1. New-Product Publicity
2. Product Placement
3. Consumer Education
4. Sponsorships
5. Internet Web Sites

Define New-Product Publicity

Instrumental in introducing new products and
services by prompting free news stories or positive word of mouth about them.

Define Product Placement

Used to reinforce brand awareness and create favorable attitudes by featuring products in
movies, TV shows, or other media.

Define Consumer Education

Aims to create better-informed and more loyal customers by providing educational content and information about products and services.

Define Sponsorships

Companies invest in supporting issues, causes, or events consistent with corporate objectives,
improving brand awareness or enhancing corporate image.

Define Internet Web Sites

Used for introducing new products, promoting
existing products, obtaining consumer feedback, communicating legislative and regulatory
information, showcasing events, providing links to related sites, and interacting with customers and potential customers.
