LO 3:
THE AIDA CONCEPT

What is AIDA

A model that outlines the process for achieving promotional goals in terms of stages of consumer involvement with the message.

What is AIDA stands for?

Attention, Interest, Desire, and Action.

How AIDA concept work?

The model suggests that a successful promotional campaign should first
1. Grab the consumer's attention
2. Generate interest in the product or service
3. Create a desire for it
4. Prompt the consumer to take action,
such as making a purchase.
