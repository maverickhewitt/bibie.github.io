LO 8:
SALES PROMOTION

List the Tools for Consumer Sales Promotion

1. Coupons and Rebates
2. Premiums
3. Loyalty Marketing Programs
4. Contests and Sweepstakes
5. Sampling
6. Point-of-Purchase (P-O-P) Promotion
7. Online Sales Promotion

Define Coupons and Rebates

Coupons: Certificates for immediate price reductions when purchasing the product.
Rebates: Offers price reduction, but the reward is delayed and requires proof of purchase.

Define Premiums

Extra items given to consumers for purchasing the promoted product, encouraging brand loyalty.

Define Loyalty Marketing Programs

Reward loyal customers for making multiple
purchases to foster long-term relationships.

Define Contests and Sweepstakes

Contests: Skill-based competitions where participants vie for prizes.
Sweepstakes: Chance-based promotions with free participation for winning rewards.

Define Sampling

Allows risk-free product trials to overcome
consumer hesitation and encourage purchase.

Define Point-of-Purchase (P-O-P) Promotion

In-store displays and promotions to attract
customers and induce impulse buying.

Define Online Sales Promotion

Effective and cost-efficient promotions online,
including free merchandise, sweepstakes, free shipping, and coupons.
