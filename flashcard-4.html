LO 4:
THE PROMOTIONAL MIX

What is the Promotional Mix?

1. Advertising
2. Public Relations
3. Personal Selling
4. Sales Promotion

Define Advertising

Any form of impersonal (one-way) paid
communication in which the sponsor or company is identified.

Define Public Relations

- marketing function that evaluates public attitudes,
- identifies areas within the organization the public
may be interested in,
- executes a program of action to earn public understanding and acceptance

Define Personal Selling

Purchase situation involving a personal,
paid-for communication between two people in an attempt to influence each other.

Define Sales promotion

consists of all marketing activities
—other than personal selling, advertising, and public relations
—that stimulate consumer purchasing and dealer effectiveness
